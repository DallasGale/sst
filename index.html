STEADY STATE THEORY

25.07.22

ATTRACTS EP released

The debut EP for Steady State Theory has been released worldwide today. An exploration of analoge synths, organ, drum machine and vocal samples. Australian musician & producer Dallas Gale has been toying with these concepts for a while now and is excited to share these these 3 tracks with you all.

For the past 20 years Dallas has been primarily played the guitarist role in The In The Out, Dead River Deeps, Deserters, The Graveyard Sons, however music production is where Dallas can express himself completely.

Sample Credits: "It attracts me & holds me" features a vocal sample from Nina Simone, as recorded at Montreux Festival on 7/3/1976. The vocal is part of her quite emotional conversation with the audience before she begins the encore. "High" features a short phrase vocal sample from Carly Simon's "The Best Thing" "What are you trying to do? features a vocal sample from the 1956 film "On the Run"
