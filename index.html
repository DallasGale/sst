STEADY STATE THEORY

29.07.22

ATTRACTS EP released

Australian electronic music producer/ musican Dallas Gale (aka Steady State Theory) has released his debut EP Attracts worldwide today. Using analogue synths, drum machines, farfissa organ and sampled voice this is the first of many recordings to come.

For the past 20 years Dallas has primarily been a guitar player in some of Melbourne's most loved rock bands including The In The Out, The Graveyard Sons, Deserters and Dead River Deeps. However it's in the studio enviornment that Dallas spends most of his nights.

"...having the freedom to do whatever style of music I want and use any sounds I come up with gives me immense freedom to create the music I want to listen to.

On this EP Dallas has introduced sampling, especially with vocals, that gives the music a unique quality because every track will have a different voice attached to it.

"for the title track of this EP I sourced a spoken word sample of Nina Simone speaking to the audience at the Montreux Festival in 1976, right before her encore. I found the emotion in her voice, and the words used, to have such as lyrical quality to them that I had to explore it into a piece of music...""
