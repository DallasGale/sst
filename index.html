STEADY STATE THEORY

29.07.22

ATTRACTS EP released

Australian electronic music producer Dallas Gale (aka Steady State Theory ) has released his debut EP today.

The Attracts EP ties together melancholic vocal sampling over the top of an optimistic layering of electronic based instrucments ( organ, analogue synth and drum machine) to create a very unique first release.

For the past 20 years, Dallas has primarily been a guitar player in some of Melbourne's most loved rock bands, including The In The Out, The Graveyard Sons, Deserters and Dead River Deeps; however, it's in the studio environment that Dallas spends most of his nights.

Produced at his home studio in Australia and mastered by George Konings in the Netherlands this EP is only the begining of more music to come.
